LOGO PICTURE FOR THE PIE

on 9-inch pie

30min-prep time

10min-cook time

40min-Total

8 servings

style="background-color:rgb(233,233,216);color:rgb(156,156,27);pdding5px;margin:0px">INGRIDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2tbs all-purpose flour
  • 3tbs cornstarch
  • 1/4tbs salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • lemons,juiced and zested
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 4 egg yolks,beaten
  • 1(9 inch) pie crust,baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbs white sugar

    • DIRECTIONS

    1. prechet oven: preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
    2. make lemon zest.
    3. cook over medium saucepan...
      • whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour,cornstarch,and juice and lemon zest.
      • cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently,untill mixture comes to a boil.
      • stir in butter.
      • place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradualy whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
      • whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
      • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stiring constantly until thick.
      • Remove from heat
      • pour filling into baked pastry shell.
    4. make maringue:in a large glass ormatal bowl...
      • whip egg whites untill foamy.
      • Addsugar gradually, and continue to whip untill stiff peaks form.
      • spread meringiue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
      Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or untill meringue is golden brown.
